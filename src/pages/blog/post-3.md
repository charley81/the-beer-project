# Beyond IPA: Exploring Lesser-Known Beer Styles

While IPAs dominate the craft beer conversation, there's a world of unique styles waiting to be discovered. Let's explore some underappreciated beer styles and find breweries that excel at them.

## Hidden Gems of the Beer World

### Gose (Pronounced "go-zuh")

**A Tart, Salty German Classic**

- Originated in Goslar, Germany
- Characterized by coriander and salt additions
- Perfect for warm weather drinking
